Mission

Built for the Community

OurSQL Foundation exists to ensure the long-term health and independence of the MySQL-compatible open source ecosystem.

⚡

Vendor Neutral

No single company controls the roadmap. Decisions are made collaboratively by the community, for the community.

🔓

Open Source First

We champion permissive licensing and open governance to keep MySQL-compatible software free and accessible forever.

🌐

Ecosystem Sustainability

Supporting maintainers, funding critical infrastructure, and fostering collaboration across the MySQL ecosystem.

🤝

Community Governance

Transparent processes, open membership, and community-driven decision-making at every level of the foundation.
