OurSQL Foundation is a vendor-neutral nonprofit dedicated to the advancement and sustainability of open source MySQL-compatible database technologies.

## Mission

We exist to ensure that the MySQL-compatible database ecosystem remains open, community-driven, and sustainable — free from the control of any single vendor.

Our work spans governance, advocacy, infrastructure support, and community building across the entire MySQL-compatible open source stack: MySQL, Percona Server, MariaDB, Vitess, TiDB, and beyond.

## Why OurSQL Foundation?

The MySQL ecosystem powers a significant portion of the world's databases. Yet the governance structures, licensing models, and sustainability mechanisms for key projects have long been fragmented or vendor-controlled.

OurSQL Foundation was created to change that — to provide a neutral home where developers, companies, DBAs, and community members can collaborate on the future of MySQL-compatible technology without fear of lock-in or agenda capture.

## Values

- **Openness** — All work happens in public. Decisions are transparent and documented.
- **Neutrality** — No single company or interest controls outcomes.
- **Sustainability** — We invest in maintainers, infrastructure, and long-term project health.
- **Inclusivity** — The MySQL community is global and diverse. We reflect that.
