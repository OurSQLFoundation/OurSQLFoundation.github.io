OurSQL Foundation is a vendor-neutral nonprofit dedicated to the advancement and sustainability of open source MySQL-compatible database technologies.

## Mission

The MySQL-compatible ecosystem — MySQL, MariaDB, Vitess, TiDB, and the tooling around them — runs a significant share of the world's production databases. Governance of that ecosystem has always been fragmented or vendor-controlled. Key decisions happen behind closed doors. Licensing shifts without community input. Maintainers carry costs that no one coordinates to share.

OurSQL Foundation is a neutral home where developers, DBAs, and organizations can work on those problems without any single vendor setting the agenda.

## What we work on

- **Governance advocacy** — pushing for open, documented decision-making across MySQL-compatible projects
- **Licensing** — advocating for permissive, stable licenses that users and contributors can rely on
- **Infrastructure** — funding shared tooling, testing environments, and critical maintenance work
- **Maintainer support** — helping sustain the people who keep these projects running

## Values

- **Openness** — all work happens in public; decisions are documented and auditable
- **Neutrality** — no single company or interest controls outcomes
- **Sustainability** — we invest in maintainers, infrastructure, and long-term project health
- **Inclusivity** — the MySQL community spans the world; the foundation reflects that

## Governance {#governance}

OurSQL Foundation operates under an open governance model. Membership, voting procedures, and working group charters will be published as the structure develops. Nothing is decided in private.

If you want to shape how the foundation is built, [open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/OurSQLFoundation/OurSQLFoundation/issues/new?template=get-involved.yml) and tell us what you'd like to work on.
